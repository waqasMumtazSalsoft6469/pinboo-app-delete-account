PinBoo Delete Account Info

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your account:

  1. Open the app and go to the Profile screen.
  2. Press the Delete Account button.
  3. An alert will appear asking for confirmation.
  4. If you're sure you want to proceed, tap on Delete Account.
  5. Your account will be permanently deleted, and you will be logged out of the app.

Note: Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone!
